Mahakavi Devkota Model Secondary School, located in Sunwal-4, Nawalparasi, Nepal, is a distinguished public educational institution offering a comprehensive range of academic programs from early childhood development (ECD) through to higher secondary (+2) levels.


Vision:


To produce skilled human resources, particularly in the field of automobile mechanics, contributing to the nation's development.
